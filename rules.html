Simon Says — Rules

Repeat the sequence of colors in the same order. Each round adds one new step.

  1. Watch the sequence shown by the game(form of flashes).
  2. Repeat the sequence by just clicking on the boxes in the same order.
  3. If you match the sequence exactly, you advance to the next round.
  4. If you make a mistake, the game ends (and if you want, you can try again).
  5. The goal is to complete as many rounds as possible — each round increases the sequence length by 1.

Tips: Pay attention to timing and rhythm of the sequence. Break long sequences into smaller chunks to memorize faster.
